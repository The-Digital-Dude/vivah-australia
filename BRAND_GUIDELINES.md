# VIVAH AUSTRALIA
## Brand Identity & Visual Guidelines
**Version 1.0 – Approved Brand Foundation**

---

## 1. Introduction

This document defines the approved brand identity and visual guidelines for Vivah Australia. It serves as the foundation for all future design, communication and marketing assets including the website, mobile application, social media, advertising, print materials and corporate documents.

The purpose is to ensure a consistent, trustworthy and premium brand experience across all platforms.

## 2. Brand Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Brand Name** | Vivah Australia |
| **Category** | Matrimonial Platform |

| **Positioning** | Australia's trusted Indian matrimonial platform that combines tradition with modern technology. |

## 8. Brand Voice

Our communication is always: **Professional, Warm, Respectful, Supportive and Positive.**

We speak with clarity and empathy. We respect traditions while embracing modern values.

### Brand Voice Keywords
- Professional
- Warm
- Supportive
- Clear
- Respectful
- Positive

### Deprecated Terminology (AI Jargon)
Avoid using robotic, overly gamified, or generic AI jargon such as:
- ❌ **"Journey"** (Use: experience, process, search, relationship)
- ❌ **"Unlock"** (Use: get access, view, join)
- ❌ **"Seamless"** (Use: easy, simple)
- ❌ **"Elevate"** (Use: improve, enhance)

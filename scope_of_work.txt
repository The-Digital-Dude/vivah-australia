Purpose
Vivah
 Australia is a secure matrimonial platform designed primarily for the Indian community in Australia, with future scalability to international markets.
The platform will provide users with the ability to create matrimonial profiles, search for suitable matches, communicate through an internal messaging system, participate in community discussions, complete profile verification processes, and manage membership subscriptions through a secure and scalable online platform.
